About Zack D. Cook

Zack D. Cook is an engineer, author, and unapologetic Florida man on a mission to blend scientific wonder with emotional storytelling.

Zack's currently working on his debut novel, Swamp Ass, The Apocalypse, and Other Inconveniences, which is set to be an urban-fantasy-meets-sci-fi romp that explores what happens when the end of the world smells faintly of sunscreen and regret.
